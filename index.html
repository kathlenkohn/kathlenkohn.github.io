Kathlén Kohn

I'm an Associate Professor in Mathematics at KTH in Stockholm studying Nonlinear Algebra. I enjoy to investigate connections of algebraic geometry to discrete geometry, combinatorics, representation theory, statistics, machine learning, computer vision, and music!
