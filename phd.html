PhD position in Applied Mathematics, specialization in Machine Learning

Application deadline • 11. March 2021 11:59 PM CET

How to applyTBA

The successful candidate will pursue a PhD project centered around the theoretical foundations of machine learning under the supervision of Joakim Andén and Kathlén Kohn. The position is a time-limited, full-time, five year position starting at an agreed upon date. The position is fully funded for four years and will be extended to five years by assigning teaching duties. The student will be enrolled in the Doctoral program in Applied and Computational Mathematics. Moreover, the successful candidate will be part of vibrant and diverse research groups in algebraic geometry and mathematical statistics as well as the newly established unit Mathematics of Data and AI. Students interested in fields related to the following are encouraged to apply: algebraic / complex / differential / discrete geometry, artificial intelligence, statistics.

The theme of the possible research projects is Convergence in Learning.
